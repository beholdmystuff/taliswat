DEFSTRING(CHARACTER_CARD_LEPRECHAUN,\
"SPECIAL ABILITIES\n\n\
You begin the game with two Spells.\n\n\
You need not roll the die in the Forest unless you wish to. If you choose to roll, you must accept the result.\n\n\
Whenever you land on the Woods, you may gain 3 gold.\n\n\
Whenever you roll a 6 for your move, you may teleport to any other space in the same Region instead of moving normally.\n\n\
Start: Forest          Alignment: Neutral")

DEFSTRING(CHARACTER_CARD_NECROMANCER,\
"SPECIAL ABILITIES\n\n\
You begin the game with two Spells.\n\n\
When you attack another character, you may choose to make the attack psychic combat. You may not do this when you are attacked by another character.\n\n\
Spirits will not attack you, although you may choose to attack them.\n\n\
If you do not attack a Spirit, you may enthral it if your Craft is higher than the Spirit's Craft. The Spirit joins you as a Follower and adds its Craft to yours in psychic combat, after which it departs to the discard pile. You may use any number of enthralled Spirits during a psychic combat.\n\n\
Start: Graveyard          Alignment: Evil")

DEFSTRING(CHARACTER_CARD_OGRE_CHIEFTAIN,\
"SPECIAL ABILITIES\n\n\
Monsters will not attack you, although you may choose to attack them.\n\n\
Whenever you defeat a Monster in battle, you may dominate it and keep it as a Follower instead of a trophy. The Monster adds its Strength to yours in battle, after which it departs to the discard pile. You may use any number of dominated Monsters during a battle.\n\n\
Start: Crags          Alignment: Neutral")

DEFSTRING(CHARACTER_CARD_WARLOCK,\
"SPECIAL ABILITIES\n\n\
You begin the game with two Spells.\n\n\
At the start of your turn, you may gain your full complement of Spells, according to your current Craft.\n\n\
Whenever you complete a Warlock Quest, you may take one card of your choice from the purchase deck for free instead of gaining a Talisman as your reward.\n\n\
Start: Warlock's Cave          Alignment: Neutral")
